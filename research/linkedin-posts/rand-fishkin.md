## Notes for Future Playbook

Rand's content suggests that modern B2B SaaS marketing should focus less on driving clicks and more on building influence. Rather than measuring success solely through website traffic, companies should prioritize audience attention, platform-native content, brand mentions, and community participation. His research consistently highlights that buyers discover brands across many channels before ever visiting a company website.
